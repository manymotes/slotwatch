How it works

Step 1

Pick your service center

Enter your email and choose your Tesla service center by city or ZIP. No Tesla login required.

Step 2

Set your window

Tell us the date range you'd take an earlier appointment in — we'll only email you about openings that fit.

Step 3

Get an email, then book

The moment an earlier opening appears at your center, we email you. Open the Tesla app to reschedule before it's gone.
